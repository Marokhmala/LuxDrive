Driving Requirements

To rent a vehicle from LuxeDrive, the driver must meet the following criteria:

  • Minimum age of 21 (25 for high-performance supercars).
  • A valid driver's license held for at least 2 years.
  • Passport or National ID for identity verification.

Insurace & Protection

All our rentals include basic insurance coverage. However, we offer premium protection packages for additional peace of mind:

  • Basic: Standard liability with a deductible.
  • Premium: Reduced deductible and coverage for glass/tires.
  • Ultimate: Zero deductible and full protection.

Fuel Policy

We operate on a "Full-to-Full" basis. You will receive the car with a full tank of fuel and must return it with a full tank. Failure to do so will result in refueling charges at premium rates.

Mileage Limits

Standard rentals include 200km per day. Additional mileage will be charged at a rate specified in your rental agreement (typically $2-5 per km depending on the vehicle class).

Late Returns

A grace period of 59 minutes is allowed. Returns beyond this time will incur an additional day's rental charge.
