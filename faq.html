Frequently Asked Questions

Home / FAQ
How do I book a car?
Simply browse our fleet on the "Cars" page, select your vehicle, choose your dates, and proceed to checkout via PayPal.
What documents do I need?
You will need a valid driver's license, a passport or national ID, and a credit card for the security deposit.
Can I return the car in a different city?
One-way rentals are available for an additional fee. Please contact our support team to arrange this before your trip.
What is included in the price?
The price includes the vehicle rental, basic insurance, and 200km of mileage per day. Fuel and extra protection packages are separate.
